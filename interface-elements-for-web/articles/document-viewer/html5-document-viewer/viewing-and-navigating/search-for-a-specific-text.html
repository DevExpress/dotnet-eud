Search for a Specific Text

To search for a specific text throughout a document, click the Search button on the right side of the Document Viewer or on the Viewer's toolbar. This switches to the Search panel.

In this panel, you can input the text to find and specify whether or not to use case-sensitive search and whether it is required to match the whole word during the search. To start searching or search again, press ENTER. You can also use the buttons to continue searching backward or forward, respectively.

In addition, the Search panel displays the search result. So, you can quickly locate the required text within a document by selecting the corresponding item in the Search Result list.
